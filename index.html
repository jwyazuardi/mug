LKY Award for Mathematics and Science Recipient: Jeremy Jee De Sheng

Temasek Secondary School (TMS) is a place of memories for me. I experienced numerous highs and lows during my four years there. Most importantly, it changed my life forever.

OCIP Participant: Dong Shihua

The 2013 Overseas Community Involvement Programme (OCIP) trip to Laos has given me much more than I have expected. I saw things that I have never seen before. This trip had not only opened my eyes but also my heart.

NCDCC Sergeant: Sharifah Sheikha

I would never have imagined myself being a member of a Uniformed Group. However, the journey has helped me grown much.

All-Rounder Temasekian offering Normal Course: Lim Jing Heng

There are a lot of opportunities to learn and experience new things in Temasek Secondary School.
